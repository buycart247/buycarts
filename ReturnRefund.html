Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping at Buycart. We value your satisfaction and want to ensure you have a smooth shopping experience.
If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help!

1. Returns

You can request a return within [7-14 days] of receiving your order.
To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • In the original packaging
  • Accompanied by the receipt or proof of purchase
Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned, such as:
  • Sale or discounted products
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Intimate or hygiene-related products (like innerwear, cosmetics, etc.)

2. Return Process

To start a return:

  • Contact us at support@ buycart.com with your order number and reason for return.
  • Our team will review your request and provide a return authorization with shipping instructions.
  • Once the item is received and inspected, we'll notify you about the status of your return.
Please Note:
Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a damaged, defective, or incorrect item.

3. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

  • If approved, a refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.
If your refund is delayed, please check:
  • Your bank or payment provider's processing time
  • Then contact us if the issue persists

4. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Notify us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Send photos or a short video showing the issue
We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost.

5. Exchange Policy

We currently offer exchanges only for defective or wrong-size items (where applicable).
Contact us to confirm stock availability before sending your item back.

6. Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled before shipment.
Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation is not possible, but you may request a return after delivery.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails
To make a request, contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about returns, refunds, or exchanges, reach out to us:

  • Email: support@buycart.com
  • Website: www.buycart.com
