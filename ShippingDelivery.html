Shipping & Delivery

Welcome to Buycart. This Shipping Policy explains how we process and deliver orders placed on our website.

1. Order Processing

  • All orders are processed within 1-3 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Orders are not processed or shipped on weekends or public holidays.
  • Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking details.

2. Shipping Time

Estimated delivery times depend on your location:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-4 business days (if available)
Delivery times may vary due to weather conditions, courier delays, or high order volumes.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on:

  • Delivery location
  • Package weight
  • Shipping method selected
Sometimes we may offer free shipping on special promotions or orders above a certain amount.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS.

You can use this tracking number to check the delivery status on the courier partner's website.

5. Delayed or Lost Packages

If your order is delayed or lost during transit:

  • Please contact us at support@buycart.com
  • We will coordinate with the shipping partner to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

6. Incorrect Shipping Address

Please ensure that your shipping address is correct when placing an order.

We are not responsible for orders delivered to incorrect addresses provided by customers.

7. Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damaged package and product
We will assist you with a replacement or refund according to our Return & Refund Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding shipping, feel free to contact us:

  • Email: support@buycart.com
  • Website: www.buycart.com
